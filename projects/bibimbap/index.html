Bibimbap:

An Illustrated Guide

Ingredients

Prep Time:

Time until eating:

carrots & radishes

1

julienne 1 carrot and 1 radish

2

place the julienned carrots and radishes into a clean pickling jar

3

combine 1 cup water, 1 cup white sugar, and 1 cup rice vinegar in a saucepan

4

boil on medium heat until the sugar dissolves

5

after the pickling liquid cools, pour it over the carrots and radishes

6

close the lid and leave the jar at room temperature for at least 3 hours

7

chill for at least an hour, but it is easier to complete this step the night before

cucumbers

1

julienne 1 cucumber

2

placed julienned cucumbers in a colander over a large bowl and salt lightly

3

toss cucumbers and let them sit for 1 hour

4

apply pressure to cucumbers, pushing as much liquid out as possible

5

place cucumbers in a medium bowl with garlic, pepper flakes, rice vinegar, seasame oil, and sugar

6

mix well

7

cover and chill for at least an hour, but it is easier to complete this step the night before

beef

1

mince 1 garlic clove

2

marinate beef with soy sauce, seasame oil, gochujang, brown sugar, and minced garlic

3

marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes as you prepare the other elements

4

heat cooking oil on high until it shimmers

5

add beef mixture

6

cook on medium to high meat until thoroughly cooked, about 5 minutes

rice

1

add 2 cups water and 1.5 cups rice to a medium saucepan

2

bring to a boil

3

cover and reduce heat to a simmer for 20 minutes

bibimbap sauce

1

mince 1 garlic clove

2

combine gochujang, rice wine vinegar, seasame oil, seasame seeds, white sugar, minced garlic, and water in a bowl

3

mix thoroughly

spinach

1

boil water in a medium saucepan

2

add salt

3

plunge spinach into boiling water for 30 seconds

4

drain boiled water and run spinach under cold water for 1 minute

5

cut spinach into 3 sections

6

mince 1 garlic clove

7

combine seasame oil, seasame seeds, minced garlic, salt, and spinach in a bowl

8

mix thoroughly

mushrooms

egg

combine & eat!
