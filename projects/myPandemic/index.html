COVID-19 will define this generation. Our relationship to the pandemic is tangled up with a seemingly simple act: staying inside. Staying home to protect ourselves and others is a complex and problematic mandate, bringing to light systemic inequalities that linger all around us.

By taking this short survey, you can record some of your COVID-19 experiences and create a visual identity for your story. Your anonymous data could reveal shared struggles and even help remake the world.
